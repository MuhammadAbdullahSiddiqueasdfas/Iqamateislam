Long Term Plan

2023

Expansion to 5 New Cities

Establish new branches in 5 major cities to spread Islamic knowledge and provide community services.

2024

Digital Platform Launch

Develop a comprehensive digital platform for online learning, research dissemination, and community engagement.

2025

International Research Conference

Host an international conference bringing together Islamic scholars and researchers from around the world.

2026

Quran Translation Project

Complete translation of the Quran into 3 additional languages with detailed commentary and contextual explanations.

2027

Educational Institute Establishment

Establish a formal educational institute offering certified courses in Islamic studies and research methodology.
