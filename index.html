Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Quran

"Deeply connected with the Quran" - Fostering a generation that lives by the teachings of the Holy Quran.

Science

"Excel in knowledge and science" - Encouraging academic excellence and scientific inquiry.

Dawah

"Committed to spreading Dawah with wisdom" - Sharing the message of Islam with compassion and understanding.

Dars

"Enriched through regular Dars that strengthens faith" - Regular spiritual learning sessions for growth.

Our Aim

"To foster a spiritually strong, academically excellent, and socially responsible student community that lives by the Quran, benefits from scientific knowledge, engages in Dawah, and regularly learns through Dars to grow in faith and character."

What We Focus On:

  • Focus on students (spiritual + academic + social growth)
  • Quran as the core source of guidance
  • Building a supportive environment (brotherhood, unity)
  • Linking Islam with modern knowledge (science, research, career skills)
  • Creating responsible Muslims who spread Dawah and benefit society

Implementation Strategy

Quran

Organize study circles, Tafsir sessions, and Quran competitions to strengthen connection with the Holy Book.

Science & Education

Encourage research, career counseling, and academic workshops to excel in knowledge.

Dawah

Train students to share Islam with wisdom through seminars, booklets, and social media outreach.

Dars

Hold regular weekly/monthly Dars for spiritual nourishment and faith strengthening.

Brotherhood & Support

Create a network where students help each other (financially, academically, emotionally).

Personal Development

Build leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills in light of Islamic values.

Community Service

Involve students in charity, social work, and volunteering to serve humanity.
