Our Mission

We are striving to make the knowledge of the Quran accessible to everyone. Our goal is to establish new branches in different cities to spread divine guidance. We believe in rewarding dedication - which is why we distribute Qurans to those who complete three sessions regularly, encouraging continued learning and practice.
